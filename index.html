Below are some short & sweet clips on how to use the app.

Make exercises

Create your own custom named exercises from the Graphs page. Just tap the Add button to get started.

Multi-delete

Long-press items to select them, and then tap the trash icon in the top right to delete multiple items.

Craft custom plans

Tap the add button from the Plans tab to get started. Pick exercises of your choice, and label or pick the day you want to complete each workout on.

Automatic timers

After entering an exercise, a timer fires off in the background. Control it manually from the timers page.

Customization

Everything is configurable! Customize to your hearts content. From any of the list views, tap the menu (right of the search bar) then access the settings. On the timers page it's the cog in the top right.

Insanely custom!

You can even remove entire tabs from the app. From the settings page tap on the tabs item. Toggle tabs on/off and use the drag handles to order them as you wish.
